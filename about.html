CossiePlay is a business run by a family, and operating in Hulkstown since 2012. We create outstanding customised costumes. Order any costumes you want, from simple Spiderman or elf costumes, and even costumes that have many features (armour, animatronics, realistic-looking weapons, and working force fields). Depending how complicated the requested costume is, the prices vary from $10 to $10.000. Costumes take 1 to 50 weeks to create

We also accept second-hand costumes, and sell them as well. Price list is available on THIS PAGE

Every first Saturday on each month, we hold a party called “CossiePlay Party” at the town hall. Entry fee is $15 for adult, $10 for kids 6-17 years, and kids under 6 years are free. Check it out On this Page
